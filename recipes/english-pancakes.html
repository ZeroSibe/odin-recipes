English Pancakes

Traditional English Pancakes

On Pancake day, English Pancakes can be seen all over the breakfast ailse in UK supermarkets. They are often confused as crepes but these are a bit more doughy.

This recipe will serve 12 pancakes in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil, plus a little extra for frying
  • 1 pinch salt
  • caster sugar to serve (optional)

Steps

  1. Put plain flour, eggs, milk, vegetable oil and salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest.
  3. Set a medium frying pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges and caster sugar, or your favourite filling.
